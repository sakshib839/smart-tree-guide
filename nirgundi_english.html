🌿 Nirgundi

(Vitex negundo)

Nirgundi is a powerful medicinal plant known for its anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving properties.

🌱 Basic Information

  • Common Name: Nirgundi
  • Scientific Name: Vitex negundo
  • Family: Lamiaceae
  • Origin: India & Southeast Asia

🌍 Environmental Importance

  • Improves soil quality
  • Acts as natural pest repellent
  • Supports biodiversity

💊 Medicinal Uses

  • Reduces joint pain & arthritis
  • Used in fever treatment
  • Heals wounds and swelling
🌟 Natural pain reliever plant
