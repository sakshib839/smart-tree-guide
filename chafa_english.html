🌸 Chafa

(Plumeria)

Chafa is a beautiful flowering tree known for its pleasant fragrance and cultural importance.

🌱 Basic Information

  • Scientific Name: Plumeria
  • Family: Apocynaceae
  • Origin: Tropical regions

🌍 Importance

  • Decorative plant
  • Used in temples
  • Provides fragrance

💊 Uses

  • Used in perfumes
  • Relieves stress
  • Used in rituals
