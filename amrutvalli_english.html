🌿 Amrutvalli

(Tinospora cordifolia)

Amrutvalli, also known as Giloy, is a powerful medicinal plant in Ayurveda. It is called the "Nectar of Life" due to its strong immunity-boosting properties.

🌱 Basic Information

  • Common Name: Amrutvalli / Giloy
  • Scientific Name: Tinospora cordifolia
  • Family: Menispermaceae
  • Origin: India, Tropical regions
  • Type: Climbing medicinal plant

🌍 Environmental Importance

  • Grows easily in tropical climates
  • Improves green cover
  • Supports ecosystem balance
  • Requires minimal maintenance

💊 Medicinal Uses

  • Boosts immunity
  • Helps fight infections
  • Controls fever (especially dengue & viral)
  • Improves digestion
  • Purifies blood

Widely used in Ayurvedic medicines

🏡 Practical Uses

  • Used in herbal juices and tonics
  • Consumed as powder or kadha
  • Used in tablets and capsules
  • Home remedy for fever and immunity
🌟 Fun Facts
• Called “Nectar of Life”
• Can grow even without soil support
• Highly valued in Ayurveda
• Used during COVID for immunity boosting
