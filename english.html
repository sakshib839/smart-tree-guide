🌿 Neem Tree

(Azadirachta indica)

Neem is a fast-growing evergreen tree widely known for its medicinal and environmental benefits.

🌱 Basic Information

  • Common Name: Neem Tree
  • Scientific Name: Azadirachta indica
  • Family: Meliaceae
  • Origin: India, Nepal, Bangladesh
  • Lifespan: 150–200 years

🌍 Environmental Importance

  • Purifies air by absorbing CO₂ and releasing oxygen
  • Acts as a natural air filter
  • Provides shade and cooling
  • Supports birds, insects, and animals

💊 Medicinal Uses

  • Leaves: Treat skin infections and wounds
  • Bark: Improves dental health
  • Oil: Used in creams, soaps, shampoos
  • Seeds: Used to make neem oil

Used in traditional medicine like Ayurveda

🏡 Practical Uses

  • Used in toothpaste, soaps, cosmetics
  • Natural pesticide for farming
  • Protects stored grains
  • Wood used for furniture and tools
🌟 Fun Facts
• Known as “Village Pharmacy”
• Strong antibacterial & antifungal properties
• Survives in harsh conditions
• Used in Indian festivals for purification
